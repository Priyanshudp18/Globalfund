Buy Order Checklist
1
Brokerage account open karo
Fidelity (zero commissions, fractional shares), Schwab, ya Interactive Brokers. 1-3 din mein verify + fund hota hai. India se IBKR best option hai.
2
$3,000 deposit karo
Bank transfer (ACH/wire) — usually free. 1-3 business days mein aata hai. Kuch brokers instant buying power dete hain.
3
SPY khareedein — $750
Ticker "SPY" search karo. Dollar-amount order $750 (fractional). Market order use karo 9:30am–4pm EST ke beech.
4
IEUR khareedein — $600
iShares Core MSCI Europe ETF. ~$74 pe ~8 full shares milenge. Easy whole-share purchase.
5
QQQ khareedein — $450
Invesco QQQ Trust. Fractional order for $450. Tech exposure ke liye must-buy.
6
INDA khareedein — $450
iShares MSCI India ETF. ~$49 pe ~9 full shares. Aapka best emerging market bet.
7
EWJ khareedein — $300
iShares MSCI Japan ETF. ~$93 pe ~3 full shares. Japan exposure complete.
8
ASHR khareedein — $150
Xtrackers Harvest CSI 300. ~$36 pe ~4 shares. China A-shares contrarian position.
9
EWZ + KURE — $150
$90 Brazil ETF (~2 shares) + $60 China Healthcare (~4 shares). Chhoti opportunistic positions.
10
$600 cash rakho — Money Market mein
